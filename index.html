WHAT IS DRM SURVEY?

DRM Survey is a crowdsource mobile application. It allows multiple users to collect data from multiple locations in the field and upload this data to a central platform. This data can be viewed and analyzed to make further informed data-driven decisions. It allows the user to create their own surveys and upload them to a central server to access on a mobile device.

STEP 1. DOWNLOAD AND INSTALL DRM SURVEY

  • Open Play Store app in your Android mobile phone

  • Search "DRM Survey"

  • Download and install

STEP 2. CREATE AN ACCOUNT

  • Open DRM Survey in your mobile phone

  • Click Register

  • Fill the inputs and submit by clicking Register

  • Check your email to activate your new account

STEP 3. DOWNLOAD THE FORM

  • Login notification will prompt if the user not login yet, fill the username and password and click Login. You can go to the download page again in the main menu by clicking "Download Survey Form"

  • Select form that you want and click "Download", it will download the form to your mobile phone

STEP 4. Surveying

  • Select form that you want

  • Fill the questions and swipe the screen or click right arrow icon to go to the next questions

  • If you have reached the end of the question form, you can end the survey by click "Save Form and Exit"

  • You can repeat these steps if you want to collect another data

STEP 5. SUBMIT DATA TO THE SERVER

  • By default, if you already have a forms the main page is a "Collect Data" page, click upload icon to go to the "Submit Collected Data" page

  • Select form you want to submit to the server and click "Send Selected"

  • Success notification will popup if it uploaded to the server

PREPARE YOUR FORM (Additional)

Go to this url http://gicdata.ddns.net/

There are three ways to have a survey form in your account

  • Use public form

  • Create your own form or duplicate form from public form

    • Create your own form: prepare your form in excel by using XLSForm standart (example excel form) and upload it to DMS server

    • Duplicate form from public form: click "Forms" menu in DMS and duplicate form that you want by click "Clone" button

    • Invite users to contribute to collect the data

  • Get invited from another user to contribute to collect the data

There are a range of outputs that will available for download once the data has been collected and uploaded to the server. The outputs data are KML, XLS, CSV and OSM.

You can analyze and view the data using many of GIS applications such as Google Earth, ArcGIS, QGIS.
